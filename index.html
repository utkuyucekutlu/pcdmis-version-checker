PC-DMIS Version & License Checker

Find the latest available SP for your SMA license expiry date

How it works: Enter your SMA (Software Maintenance Agreement) license expiry date. The tool will show the latest Service Pack available for each PC-DMIS version that was released on or before your expiry date — meaning you are entitled to download it.

How to find your expiry date: Go to CLM and view installed licenses. The expiry date is shown under "Expiration Date".
Please enter a valid date (DD/MM/YYYY)

Example: 15/11/2022 → shows the latest SP per version released on or before that date
